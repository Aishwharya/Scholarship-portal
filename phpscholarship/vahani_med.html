Objective of Vahani Scholarship

The main aim behind launching this scheme is to open doors for the exceptionally brilliant underprivileged children in India and send them to the top universities across India for under-graduation degree courses.

Vahani Scholarship Benefits

The beneficiary will get a full tuition fee for an undergraduate degree course plus accommodation, plus a stipend for a laptop and study materials and a round-trip travel ticket.

Beneficiaries will get Intensive counselling consisting of personal skill development, application guidance and leadership development

After completion of the course, beneficiaries will get internship opportunities, career counsellors & pairing with working professionals mentorship by older students, internal guidance, debates, get-togethers, workshops, English classes, computer classes, psychological counselling and cultural adjustment through training and exchange programmes

Eligibility Criteria

The applicant should be an Indian citizen

Applicant must have passed 11th standard examination

He/She must have scored 85% or above in 10th and 11th Board Examinations

Applicant must have a desire to take admission in any undergraduate degree programme in any top Government University or Government Recognised Universities/Colleges.

The annual income of the family should not be more than 1.5 lac per annum

Students have to maintain the score in the 12th board examination and once at the university level as well to get the benefits.

Documents Required

To upload, the applicant needs scanned copy of Signature

Income certificate of family

Photograph

For interview, the applicant needs original 10th and 11th Marksheet

Income certificate issued that year

Certificates (those mentioned in the form by the applicant)

2 passport size photos
