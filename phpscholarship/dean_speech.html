Dean Ritter Scholarship

About

The Dean Ritter Foundation Scholarship for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing is privately funded by the Dean Ritter Trust.

Each year the scholarship committee will select graduating high school seniors throughout the state of Illinois who have hearing impairments who wish to continue their education at an accredited university or technical school to receive tuition scholarships.

The number and amount of scholarships varies each year.

Qualifications

You must be a resident of the state of Illinois

You must currently be a Senior in high school in good standing

You must have significant (severe to profound) hearing loss in one or both ears

The Dean Ritter Foundation will give special consideration to those with financial need, but scholarship decisions are not made solely on a financial basis.
