Eligibility Criteria For Bharati Scheme For Education

Check the eligibility details thoroughly from this section.

Bharathi Scheme for Education

Applying candidates family should belong to Brahmin Community

Also, students need to be residents of Andhra Pradesh State

Those who are studying from 1st Class to Post Graduate can quickly apply for the BSE AP Scheme

In previous year education, all the candidates need to pass in all the subjects

The parent’s family income must not be more than Rs. 3 Lakhs per year

Via regular medium, those who finish their education will award this scholarship

Need to score at least 50% marks in their previous exams

Those who are in 1st-year Btech and planning to apply for this scheme need to have an EAMCET rank below 50000.

Selection Procedure for BSE AP

Well, the complete selection of the candidates is purely based on academic performance.

Steps To Apply For Bharati Scheme For Education

To apply for this scheme all the candidates need to follow the below-mentioned points.

First of all, directly visit Andhra Pradesh Brahmin Welfare Corporation @ www.andhrabrahmin.ap.gov.in

The AP Brahmin Welfare Schemes main page is available now

You can get the Services to option on the top

So, make your pointer on it you will get three options (Schemes, Know your status, Status through SMS)

Now choose the Schemes option

You will redirect to a new page

And the page shows you all the latest available schemes

Now, find for BSE AP Scheme

The page shows you complete eligibility details

Without late read to all the information

Eligible students need to click on the Apply Online link

Now, enter all the valid data attentively

Upload the valid documents

Submit before the end date

Also, take a print for reference purposes.

Steps To Check BSE AP Online Status

To check the status all the candidates must visit Andhra Pradesh Brahmin Welfare Corporation @ www.andhrabrahmin.ap.gov.in

The main page is available now

Now take a look at the service section and choose to Know your status option

The page shows you a lot of information such as Select your option, reference number, mobile number, aadhar number, financial year, criteria number, date of birth. Hence, give all the details correctly

After entering all the details once rechecked carefully

Now, press on the Fetch option

Finally, the status available on the screen

Without late checkout and take the printout.
