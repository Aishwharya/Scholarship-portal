Description

Betty G.Miller Fellowship Award

The Betty G. Miller Fellowship Award was named in her honor; it provides financial assistance to deaf women pursuing doctorate degrees

For those who is either a graduating high school senior or prior graduate without college (GED and Home School not eligible) or, current college undergraduate.

For the GPA, Current or prior high school graduate minimum 3.4 GPA, College Undergraduate minimum GPA 3.0.

Eligibility Requirements

Must be an undergraduate student

Must attend a university or a four-year college

Restricted to residents of Oregon

Restricted to students studying Engineering/Technology, Mathematics, Science, Technology, and Society
