Eligibility Criteria

The candidates who wish to avail the benefits of this scholarship scheme must fulfill certain eligibility conditions. Find below the details of those conditions that determine who can apply for this HDFC scholarship.

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship in School Programme (Merit-Cum-Means Based)

Open for Indian students who are currently studying in Class 6 to 12 in a private, government or government-aided school.

They must have passed the previous qualifying examination with at least 55% marks.

Annual family income must be less than or equal to INR 2.5 lakh (2,50,000).

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship Beyond School Programme (Merit-Cum-Means Based)

Open for Indian students who are pursuing non-professional courses like BA, BCom, MA, MCom, etc., at Undergraduate or Postgraduate level in recognized colleges or universities.

The students pursuing Diploma/Polytechnic courses after Class 10 or 12 can also apply.

They must have passed the previous qualifying examination with at least 55% marks.

Annual family income must be less than or equal to INR 2.5 lakh (2,50,000).

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship Professional Education Programme (Merit-Cum-Means Based)

Open for Indian students who are pursuing professional courses like BBA, BTech, BCA, MBBS, MBA, MCA, MTech, etc., at Undergraduate or Postgraduate level in recognized colleges or universities in India.

They must have passed the previous qualifying examination with at least 55% marks.

Annual family income must be less than or equal to INR 2.5 lakh (2,50,000).

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship in School Programme (Need Based)

Open for Indian students who are studying in Class 6 to 12 in private, government or government-aided schools.

They must be facing a personal or family crisis that has occurred during the past three years, due to which they are unable to continue bearing the cost of education and are at risk of dropping out.

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship Beyond School Programme (Need Based)

Open for Indian students who are pursuing non-professional courses like BA, BCom, MA, MCom, etc., at Undergraduate or Postgraduate level in recognized colleges or universities.

The students pursuing Diploma/Polytechnic courses after Class 10 or 12 can also apply.

They must be facing a personal or family crisis that has occurred during the past three years, due to which they are unable to continue bearing the cost of education and are at risk of dropping out.

HDFC Bank Parivartan’s ECS Scholarship Professional Education Programme (Need Based)

Open for Indian students pursuing professional courses like BBA, BTech, BCA, MBBS, MBA, MCA, MTech, etc., at Undergraduate or Postgraduate level in recognized colleges or universities.

They must be facing a personal or family crisis that has occurred during the past three years, due to which they are unable to continue bearing the cost of education and are at risk of dropping out.
