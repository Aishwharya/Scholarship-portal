NTPC Scholarship Scheme 2022 Eligibility Criteria:

Following are some of the conditions to be eligible for the NTPC Scholarship Scheme 2022:

A candidate must be pursuing a 2nd year of B.E./ B.Tech program from specified institutes.

The age should be of the applicant must not exceed 40 years.

He/ she must be belonging to SC/ ST and PC category.

The candidate should be domicile in Rajasthan, Delhi, J&K, Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Chandigarh, and Punjab.

An applicant must not be receiving financial support from many sources.

NTPC Scholarship 2022 Rewards:

Candidates selected for the NTPC scholarship will receive INR 1500/- per month for the entire duration of the program.

How To Apply For NTPC Scholarship Scheme Application Form:

Students can apply for NTPC Scholarship Scheme 2022 by following these simple steps:

The application process will be offline.

Candidates can get the application form from the head of the institution or its authorized representative.

Application form can also be downloaded from www.ntpc.co.in

Fill in all the details correctly.
