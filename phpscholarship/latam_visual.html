LATAM International Coursework Scholarship

Eligibility

Scholarship Link

To be eligible for the Scholarship, an applicant must meet all of the following criteria

Must be a citizen of a country in the Latin America region

Must be an international student (not an Australian citizen, New Zealand citizen or permanent resident of Australia)

Must meet all admission requirements for the selected UTS postgraduate coursework program and be admitted based on a completed tertiary qualification comparable to an Australian Bachelor degree or higher

Must meet all admission requirements for the selected UTS postgraduate coursework program

Must be commencing studies at UTS full-time on campus

Must not be in receipt of any other scholarship from UTS

Must not be enrolled in a UTS Transnational Program.

Selection process

Academic merit in a completed tertiary qualification recognised by UTS from a country other than Australia for entry into the selected UTS postgraduate coursework program.

How to apply

Applications for this scholarship are not required as all eligible students will be considered.
